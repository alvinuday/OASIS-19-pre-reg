About Rocktaves

For over two decades, Rocktaves has been one of the most prestigious semi-professional band competitions in India. The flagship event of Oasis, BITS-Pilani, one of the oldest cultural festivals in the country. The rock competition has acted as a springboard for India’s premiere rock groups of the stature of Parikrama, Indian Ocean, Superfuzz, PRESTORIKA, Them Clones and Euphoria who have taken part in the competition at different points in its history.
In a bid to enlarge the talent pool, auditions are going to be held in Bengaluru, Kolkata, Kochi, Guwahati, Mumbai, Chennai, Delhi and Kathmandu making the event accessible to the largest possible set of competitors.
The winners of these rounds not only get an all-expenses paid trip to Pilani, but also a chance to compete and win cash prizes to the tune of over one lakh rupees and an album label among many other prizes.
